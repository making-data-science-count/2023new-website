    Project Introduction

    The full title of the study is: Broadening Participation in Introductory Computer Science: Investigating Self-Assessment Practices for Increasing Student Learning and Self-Efficacy in Two Institutional Contexts. The purpose is to understand how students develop their process of working on computer science projects, and to test a new approach to help students.
